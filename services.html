Story writing and dialogue editing

Fighting game stories are known to be mostly excuse plots, with some relevant exceptions. It might be hard to build a coherent narrative that showcases all characters without feeling forced... and this is where I come into play. World-building? Dialogue writing? Story pacing? You can contact me for a first consultancy free of charge. All the relevant details in the practical infographic below!

Story writing

  • 1 call with the client to define the game's setting (I'll sign any required NDA)
  • once I have received the game's "bible" (a.k.a. a design document) I will prodcue 1 free story chapter
  • if the quality/tone is okay, we will proceed by discussing the deadlines/payment
  • if the quotation is not accepted, the free chapter can't be used in the finished product
  • I will transfer all theIP rights for all entities created for the project to the customer

Moveset design

Sometimes, deciding which move might fit a character isn't an easy process. How to make each character unique? How to underline their strengths and their weaknesses through their movesets? How to showcase their personalities just by means of their motion? This is where I offer you my expertise! Consultancy rates depend on the level of detail required.

Moveset design

  • 1 call with the client to define the game's tone (realistic, rule-of-cool, shonen anime)
  • 1 brainstorming session with the client about the character + all design notes
  • after this, I will produce 3 normal moves and 2 special moves for free + sketches or 3D animations to help visualize them
  • if the client is happy with them, the next step is discussing deadlines/payment
  • the free moves provided cannot be used if the offer is declined
  • different service tiers:
    rough thematic design    frame data
    design of full, specific moves    additional properties
