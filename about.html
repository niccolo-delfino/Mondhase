About

Welcome to the far side of the Moon

My name is Andrea Demetrio - video game developer and writer for SuperCombo.gg. With four years of experience in the indie fighting game community, both as a developer and a reporter, I have decided to offer my help to other game makers that might be stuck on some design issues.

Do you need a story writer for avoiding the "evil guy holds martial arts tournament" excuse plot? Would you like to have some clever dialogue options and meaningful character interactions? Are you planning to flesh out your world but you don't know where to start? This is the right place for you!

Are you having troubles in defining which moves to give to your cool characters? Need ideas for a super? Stuck on deciding which frame data to apply to which move? I can help there too!
